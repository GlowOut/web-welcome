Payment Processing Fees
  • Card payments: 1.5% + 20p
  • Klarna (Pay Later): 4.99% + 35p

We partner with Stripe for secure card payments, and they charge a processing fee on each card payment. For example, on a £50 service the fee is ~£0.95 (1.5% + 20p), so you receive ~£49.05. No extra fees from GlowOut.

Fees may vary by card type and region. Exact charges are set by Stripe.

Our Features

This list is forever growing bigger. You want something added? Let us know and we'll get it added to our list of amazing features!

Take Card Payments

We're partnered with Stripe. Meaning that you can enable card payments for your services!

Take Deposits Before Booking

Choose a total amount or a percentage of the service cost to ensure your time is respected, even with last minute dropouts and no shows!

Partner Panel Access

Your very own Partner Panel gives you access to service performance graphs, revenue tracking, your top rated services and upcoming appointments.

Booking Calendar

Your fully automated bookings calendar keeps track of every past, present and future appointments. With access to complete booking information including invoices.

Client Books

Automatically keep track of every client who has booked one of your services. View their completed and upcoming bookings and their contact information if needed.

Employee Creation

Work in a team? You can create, track and manage your employees all in one place. You'll also have a list of every detailed service carried out by your employee.

Unlimited Services

Add as many services as you like to your Partner profile, including descriptions, images, time taken and pricing. You can also hide services if you want to take a break.

Your Own Gallery

Gain access to your own gallery to showcase your amazing work! Upload as many images as you like to attract new clients.

Client & Revenue Reports

We automatically generate reports on your revenue and client base, giving you who, what and when for the clients and full income reporting. Downloadable via either pdf or csv.

Client Reviews

Everyone needs helpful feedback. We encourage reviews so that more and more people will book your services with confidence! We also have review reporting for those less than flattering comments.

Link your Socials

Who doesn't love more followers? Link your socials to your profile so that your clients can like, follow and keep up to date with your FB & Insta profiles.

Much, MUCH More!

We're new in town, so we're forever adding new features. Anything that will make your life easier, let us know and we'll add it just for you 💕
