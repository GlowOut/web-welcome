Our Features

This list is forever growing bigger. You want something added? Let us know and we'll get it added to our list of amazing features!

Take Card Payments

We're partnered with Stripe. Meaning that you can enable card payments for your services!

Take Deposits Before Booking

We're building in a deposit system just for you. Choose a total amount or a percentage of the service cost to ensure your time is respected, even with last minute dropouts and no shows!

Owner Panel Access

Your very own Owner Panel gives you access to service performance graphs, revenue tracking, your top rated services and upcoming appointments

Booking Calendar

Your fully automated bookings calendar keeps track of every past, present and future appointments. With access to complete booking information including invoices

Client Books

Automatically keep track of every client who has booked one of your services. You can view their completed and upcoming bookings and their contact information if needed

Employee Creation

Salon Subscription: Work in a team? You can create, track and manage your employees all in one place. You'll also have a list of every detailed service carried out by your employee

Unlimited Services

Add as many services as you like to your Owner profile, including descriptions, images, time taken and pricing. You can also hide services if you want to take a break

Your Own Gallery

Gain access to your own gallery to showcase your amazing work! Upload as many images as you like to attract new clients

Client & Revenue Reports

We automatically generate reports on your revenue and client base, giving you who, what and when for the clients and full income reporting. Downloadable via either pdf or csv

Client Reviews

Everyone needs helpful feedback. We encourage reviews so that more and more people will book your services with confidence! We also have review reporting for those less than flattering comments

Link your Socials

Who doesn't love more followers? Link your socials to your profile so that your clients can like, follow and keep up to date with your FB & Insta profiles

Much, MUCH More!

We're new in town, so we're forever adding new features. Anything that will make your life easier, let us know and we'll add it just for you 💕
